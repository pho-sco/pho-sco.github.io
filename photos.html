Photography

I love to cycle long distances on days with great weather. I always got one of my cameras with me when I'm on the road. Therefore, I'm always ready to take snapshots of interesting discoveries I make. This made appreciate the beauty of nature but also the creativity of mankind. It helped me to sharpen my view and to always keep an eye on the details. Things which appear obvious and normal at first often turn out to be beautiful when observed in the right manner. Please see a small selection of the photos I took in 2021 with two different cameras: A Canon EOS M50 digital camera and a Canon A-1 film camera.
